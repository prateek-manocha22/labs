# 🌱 Form Filler (Foundation)

## 📝 Overview
Build an intelligent form-filling assistant that extracts relevant information from documents and populates structured forms automatically.

- **Expected Output**: Drag & drop document → auto-filled form fields with confidence scores.
- **Why it Matters**: Manual data entry costs SMBs thousands of hours per year.
