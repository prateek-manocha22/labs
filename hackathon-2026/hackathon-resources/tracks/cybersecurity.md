# 🌱 Cybersecurity (Foundation)

## 🛡️ Overview
Build an agentic AI system that can detect threats, investigate anomalies, autonomously respond to cyberattacks, and continuously evolve defense strategies by learning from new attack patterns.

- **Expected Output**: A threat detection and response system with autonomous investigation, real-time alerts, and adaptive defense strategies powered by agentic AI.
- **Why it Matters**: Cyberattacks are growing in scale and sophistication — agentic AI that continuously learns and adapts can stay ahead of threats and protect critical systems.
